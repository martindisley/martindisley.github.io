Martin Disley (born 1994) is a composer and new media artist based in Edinburgh, Scotland. Since graduating from the University of Edinburgh (2017) and Universitat Pompeu Fabra, Barcelona (2018) he has continued to pursue interdisciplinary practice including [composition](https://www.martindisley.co.uk/portfolio/1_restnowdiehard/), [installation](https://www.martindisley.co.uk/portfolio/3_elquequeda/) and [new media](https://www.martindisley.co.uk/portfolio/2_thelisttweeted/) projects. Exhibitions and collaborations have already taken his work to amongst other locations, [Germany, Spain and The Gambia](https://www.martindisley.co.uk/live-action/).
